Medical Engineering Modeling Ontology

Safety evidence should survive system change.

MEMO is an architecture-centered evidence backbone for medical device software: typed safety meaning, computable checks, and reviewable model views.

The problem

Evidence drifts when engineering work is split across tools.

Risk files, requirements, architecture notes, tests, reviews, and release records often change on different cadences. After a code, configuration, interface, or behavior change, teams must still explain what changed, what it can affect, and which evidence must be refreshed.
