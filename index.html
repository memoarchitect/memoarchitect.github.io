Medical Engineering Modeling Ontology

Safety evidence should stay true when the system changes.

Medical device teams can change code, configuration, interfaces, or behavior faster than the safety case can be manually reconciled. MEMO turns that reconciliation problem into a versioned model: typed safety meaning, computable checks, and reviewable views.

The problem

The safety case fails quietly when evidence loses context.

Risk files, requirements, architecture notes, tests, reviews, and release records often change on different cadences. After a code, configuration, interface, or behavior change, teams must still defend one complete thread: what changed, what it can affect, where the control is implemented, and which evidence is still fresh.
